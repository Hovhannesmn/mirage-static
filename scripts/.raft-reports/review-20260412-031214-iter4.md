ISSUE:
  FILE: data.js
  LINE: 100
  CURRENT: fullDesc: '...Նրբin պoza ու նazik demq...'

           (decoded: Ն-ր-բ-ա-գ-ե-ղ + space + կ-ե-ծ-վ-ա-ծ-ք)
  REASON: Two problems in the same phrase:
          1) "Нrbin" (Ն-ր-բ-ի-ն) is not a recognized Armenian word.
             The correct adjective for "elegant/graceful" is "Нrbagel"
             (Ն-ր-բ-ա-գ-ե-ղ). "Нurb" (Ն-ու-ր-բ = delicate/subtle)
             loses its vowel ու only in the compound prefix "Нрб-";
             the standalone suffix "-ин" does not form a valid word.
          2) "Пoза" (պ-ո-զ-ա) is a Russian/French loanword (поза/pose).
             The standard Eastern Armenian term is "Кецвацк"
             (կ-ե-ծ-վ-ա-ծ-ք = posture/stance).
