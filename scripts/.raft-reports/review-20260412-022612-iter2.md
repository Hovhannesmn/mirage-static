  FIXED: Վeradadarnal havaqaĎu
  REASON: "Վeradadarnal" (to return) is intransitive and cannot take a direct object. The definite suffix -ն makes "havaqaĎun" read as an accusative object ("return the collection"), which is ungrammatical.
